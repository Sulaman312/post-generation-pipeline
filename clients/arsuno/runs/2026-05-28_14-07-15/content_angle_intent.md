# Social Media Strategy Proposal

## Primary Intent
**Build Trust** - Position the agency as the solution to founders' operational pain points while demonstrating understanding of their winter struggles.

## Post Format
**Carousel** - Multi-slide format to break down the manual operations problem and showcase specific AI automation solutions.

## Primary Angle Statement
Winter is the perfect time for startup founders to automate their manual processes instead of burning out on repetitive tasks - here's how AI can give you back 20+ hours per week.

## Alternative Angles

• **The Winter Productivity Reset**: "While Wyoming winters keep you indoors, use this downtime to set up AI systems that'll scale your startup when spring arrives"

• **Founder Burnout Prevention**: "Stop doing $10/hour tasks when you could be focusing on $1000/hour strategy - here are 5 operations you can automate today"

• **Seasonal Scaling Strategy**: "Smart founders use Q1 to build the systems that make Q2-Q4 explosive - here's your winter automation roadmap"
