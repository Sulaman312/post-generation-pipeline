# Image Generation Prompts

## MASTER Prompt
A focused startup founder working late at night in a modern home office during winter, surrounded by multiple glowing computer screens displaying automation dashboards, AI interfaces, and workflow diagrams. Through a large window behind them, snow is gently falling outside with warm interior lighting contrasting the cold blue winter night. The founder appears relieved and energized rather than stressed, with clean charts and graphs floating holographically above their workspace showing upward trends and automated processes. The scene conveys transformation from chaos to organized efficiency. Warm golden interior lighting mixed with cool blue screen glows. Color palette: deep blues for winter night, warm golds and oranges for interior comfort, bright greens for success indicators. Photorealistic, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field focusing on the founder with screens and floating data visualizations in sharp detail.

## Instagram Variant (Portrait-Optimized)
Portrait-oriented composition of a startup founder at their desk during a Wyoming winter night, looking up from their laptop with satisfaction as holographic automation workflows float above their workspace. Snow visible through a window in the upper portion of the frame. The founder sits in the lower two-thirds of the image with glowing AI dashboard screens creating warm lighting on their face. Floating digital elements showing automated processes, time savings indicators, and efficiency metrics hover in the space above. Winter storm outside contrasts with the warm, organized digital workspace inside. Vertical framing emphasizes the transformation from overwhelming manual work (cluttered desk elements at bottom) to streamlined automation (clean floating interfaces above). Color palette: winter blues fading to warm amber workspace lighting, with bright green automation success indicators.

Multiple monitors displaying AI automation dashboards span across the right side. The founder appears calm and in control, gesturing toward floating holographic interfaces showing workflow automations, time-saving metrics, and operational efficiency charts. The composition balances the cold winter environment (left third) with the warm, technology-enabled workspace (right two-thirds). Professional lighting setup with natural window light mixing with warm desk lamps and screen glows. Color palette: cool winter grays and blues transitioning to warm workspace oranges and golds, with bright green and blue accent colors for the AI automation interfaces. Photorealistic, corporate-friendly aesthetic with clean, modern design elements.
