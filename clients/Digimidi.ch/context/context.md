# Digimidi — Company Profile Summary

## Overview
- **Company:** Digimidi SÀRL — Swiss LLC, based in Saint-Prex, Switzerland.
- **Founded:** 1995 (30 years of operation).
- **Founder:** Dr. Antoine Adam — practicing veterinarian since 1991 who uses the software daily in his own clinic. Hybrid clinical + IT expertise.
- **Mission:** Give modern veterinary practices integrated, AI-enhanced tools that optimize clinic management, improve care, and enhance experience for vets and pet families.
- **Vision:** Become the leading vet practice management platform in French-speaking Europe.
- **Philosophy:** *Built by veterinarians, for veterinarians.*

## Core Value Proposition
Consolidates all clinic management into one AI-powered platform so vets spend less time on admin and more on animal care.

**Unique selling points:** vet-founded authenticity · AI-powered efficiency (vet-specific voice-to-text) · true all-in-one (replaces 5–10 tools) · Swiss-optimized (multilingual, nLPD, CHF) · modern comms (WhatsApp/SMS) · cloud accessibility.

## Product & Pricing
- **Product:** Digimidi Practice Management Platform.
- **Price:** 129 CHF/month (currently 20% launch discount) — all features, no per-user or hidden fees.

## Feature Set (at a glance)
- **Medical records:** EHR, AI voice-to-text documentation, templates & protocols, dosage calculators.
- **Scheduling:** multi-vet calendar, 24/7 online booking, automated WhatsApp/SMS reminders, waitlist management.
- **Client communication:** multi-channel (WhatsApp/SMS/email/portal), pet-owner web & mobile apps, post-op follow-ups.
- **Documentation:** paperless forms, legally binding e-signatures, pre-appointment form delivery.
- **Imaging:** integrated DICOM viewer (X-ray, ultrasound, CT), centralized image storage.
- **Billing & finance:** automated Swiss-VAT-compliant invoicing, revenue tracking, inventory management.
- **Feedback:** automated NPS surveys, review management.
- **Analytics:** real-time KPI dashboard, custom reports, multi-location management.
- **Team & language:** role-based permissions, internal messaging; full platform in FR/DE/IT/EN.

## Ideal Customer Profile
- **Geography:** primary Romandie; secondary German-speaking Switzerland; tertiary Ticino & French border regions.
- **Size:** 2–10 vets, 5–20 staff, 50–300+ appts/week, CHF 500k–3M revenue.
- **Type:** small-animal, mixed, specialty, emergency, and multi-location groups.
- **Pain points:** legacy/paper systems, disconnected tools, admin overload, high no-shows, no online booking.
- **Decision unit:** owner decides; practice manager, senior vets, head receptionist influence.

## Competitive Advantages
Founder-market fit · 30-year track record · Swiss-first design · AI integration leadership · modern comms channels · true all-in-one · transparent pricing · cloud architecture · client-centric (pet-owner apps).

## Competitive Landscape
- **Direct (legacy EU/Swiss):** VetZ, Vetera, Provet Cloud, EasyVet — weak UX, no AI, no WhatsApp.
- **Global/US:** Avimark, IDEXX Neo, ezyVet, Impromed — Digimidi wins on Swiss fit, support, language, price.
- **Indirect:** generic software (Clinicminds, Odoo) and paper/status quo.

**Messaging pillars:** Built by vets for vets · Reclaim your time · Modern tools, exceptional care · One platform, everything you need · Swiss quality, Swiss values.

## Tech, Roadmap & Defensibility
- **Tech:** cloud SaaS, multi-tenant, nLPD-compliant, encrypted, Swiss data residency, DICOM + WhatsApp/SMS APIs.
- **Roadmap:** more integrations, predictive analytics & diagnostic support, telemedicine, loyalty/marketing automation.
