# Simonetti Freres — writing guidelines

## Style rules

- Write in clear, polished English with a professional local-service tone.
- Lead with the practical need: light, safety, privacy, comfort, layout, or finish.
- Mention bespoke measurement and careful installation when relevant.
- Keep paragraphs short and easy to scan.
- Use concrete project examples.

## Social content

- Facebook: friendly, community-oriented, approachable.
- LinkedIn: professional, trust-building, more structured and advisory.
- Google Business: concise, service-focused, local SEO-friendly.

## Article structure

- Open with the client need and project type.
- Explain why bespoke matters.
- Break down relevant solutions and use cases.
- Include practical guidance and soft CTAs.
- End with a clear invitation to contact Simonetti Freres.
