# Simonetti Freres — brand voice

## Voice

Simonetti Freres should sound professional, precise, reassuring, and craftsmanship-led. The brand voice should feel like an experienced local glazier advising a homeowner, architect, property manager, or business owner with clarity and care.

## Tone attributes

- Expert but accessible
- Elegant and practical
- Local and service-oriented
- Calm, precise, and trustworthy
- Focused on bespoke work, measurement, finish, and careful installation

## What to emphasize

- Custom glass solutions adapted to each space
- Advice, measurement, finish selection, and installation quality
- Since 1992 / long-standing expertise when useful
- Light, safety, privacy, elegance, and practical everyday use
- Projects such as shower partitions, glass railings, interior partitions, glazed doors, mirrors, sandblasted glass, and glazing replacement

## What to avoid

- Overly technical glass jargon without explanation
- Aggressive sales language
- Generic renovation copy that could apply to any contractor
- Overpromising exact outcomes without project assessment
