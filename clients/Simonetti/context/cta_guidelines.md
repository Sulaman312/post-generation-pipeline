# Simonetti Freres — CTA guidelines

## CTA approach

CTAs should invite the reader to discuss a concrete glass project. Keep them simple, local, and low-pressure.

## Recommended CTAs

- Contact us for a quote.
- Have a project? Let us discuss it.
- Request advice for your custom glass project.
- Talk to us about your space and desired finish.
- Ask for a custom solution adapted to your project.
- Discover our services.

## Best CTA placement

- Social posts: one clear final CTA plus website link.
- Google Business posts: direct contact or quote CTA.
- Articles: contextual CTAs after major sections and a stronger closing CTA.

## Avoid

- Urgency that feels artificial.
- Discount-led messaging.
- Claims that a solution is suitable before the project has been assessed.
