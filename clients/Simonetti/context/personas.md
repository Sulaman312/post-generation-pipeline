# Simonetti Freres — personas

## Homeowner planning a bespoke interior project

Wants an elegant, practical glass solution for a bathroom, staircase, living space, entrance hall, or mirror installation. Needs reassurance on measurement, finish, installation, and durability.

## Renovation client with constraints

Has unusual dimensions, an older property, or a specific layout issue. Needs custom work rather than a standard solution.

## Architect / interior designer / property professional

Looks for clean execution, reliable advice, and a glass partner capable of adapting solutions to design constraints and client expectations.

## Local business owner

May need glass partitions, glazed doors, mirrors, or glazing replacement for commercial premises. Values reliability, professional finish, and minimal disruption.
