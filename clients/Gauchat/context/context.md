Company description: A. Gauchat SA is a family-owned transport and logistics company based in Bussigny, specialising in machine transport, vehicle and equipment rental, and site logistics for construction and civil engineering projects. Founded in 1961 by Mr. Gauchat and passed on to his children, the company has built over 60 years of field expertise while maintaining the human values and reliability of a trusted family business.

Machine and equipment transport (heavy machinery, specific equipment)
Skip rental (standard skips, insulated pear-shaped skips for asphalt/bituminous mixes)
Crane truck rental (with or without operator)
Special transport and tailor-made logistics solutions
Vehicle provision for civil engineering and road construction sites (thermos, skips, crane trucks)
Site logistics coordination and planning
