# A. Gauchat SA — writing guidelines

## Style rules

- Lead with the site/logistics problem.
- Keep wording concrete: transport, rotations, access, loads, vehicles, safety, timing.
- Mention location and service area when relevant: Bussigny and French-speaking Switzerland.
- Use lists for operational benefits.
- Avoid abstract business language.

## Social content

- LinkedIn: professional, operational, construction/civil-engineering focused.
- Google Business: concise, service-led, quote/contact oriented.
