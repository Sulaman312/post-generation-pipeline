# A. Gauchat SA — brand voice

## Voice

A. Gauchat SA should sound reliable, field-tested, practical, and professional. The brand voice should reflect a family business with more than 60 years of transport and site logistics expertise.

## Tone attributes

- Direct and operational
- Professional and grounded
- Safety-conscious
- Reliable and local
- Human, but not casual

## What to emphasize

- Family business founded in 1961
- Based in Bussigny, serving French-speaking Switzerland
- 14 professionals, qualified drivers, dedicated mechanic
- Machine transport, skip rental, crane trucks, insulated pear-shaped skips, special transport, and site logistics
- Safety, reactivity, field coordination, deadlines, and tailored solutions
- Certified training company for HGV driver apprentices

## Avoid

- Overly corporate logistics jargon
- Vague promises about speed or capacity
- Casual language that weakens safety/reliability positioning
