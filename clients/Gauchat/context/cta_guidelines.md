# A. Gauchat SA — CTA guidelines

## CTA approach

CTAs should invite site managers, construction companies, and civil engineering teams to discuss a concrete logistics need.

## Recommended CTAs

- Contact us for a quote.
- Tell us about your site.
- Let's discuss your transport needs.
- Plan your logistics with us.
- Ask for advice on the right vehicle or solution.
