# Albert Descloux SA — brand voice

## Voice

Albert Descloux SA should sound precise, technical, practical, and service-oriented. The brand voice should speak to workshops, industrial clients, wood professionals, and machining teams that care about cutting quality, tool life, and reliable service.

## Tone attributes

- Technical but understandable
- Diagnostic and practical
- Precise and workshop-oriented
- Reliable and professional
- Helpful, not overcomplicated

## What to emphasize

- Sharpening, tool diagnosis, geometry checks, and cleaning
- Before/after results when available
- Machining symptoms: overheating, vibration, noise, poor surface quality
- PVD/CVD coating when it improves tool life and stability
- Collection and delivery to simplify logistics
- Service in French-speaking Switzerland, Fribourg, Gruyere, and Corbieres when relevant
