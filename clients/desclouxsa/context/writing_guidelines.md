# Albert Descloux SA — writing guidelines

## Style rules

- Lead with a workshop symptom or production issue.
- Explain the likely tool-related cause simply.
- Give a practical next step.
- Use diagnostic checklists where useful.
- Keep technical claims grounded and avoid overpromising.

## Social content

- Facebook / Instagram: clear before/after, accessible explanation, practical CTA.
- LinkedIn: more technical, production-focused, useful for workshop and operations audiences.
- Google Business: concise, symptom + service + contact.
