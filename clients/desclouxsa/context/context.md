## Key themes

### February — Before/after sharpening of a saw blade

Use symptoms such as chipping, burning, excessive force, and noise. Explain that sharpening plus geometry check and cleaning can restore cleaner cuts and improve finish. CTA: send a photo of the blade, material, and symptom.

### April — Machining: 3 signs sharpening is needed

Core signs: overheating, vibrations/noise, and loss of surface quality. Explain that sharpening plus geometry check can stabilise production and reduce rejects. Article topic: "Machining: tool heating, vibrating or marking? 3 signs that sharpening is needed."

### June — PVD/CVD coating

Use coating as a performance lever when rapid wear, build-up, edge rounding, demanding machining conditions, or frequent stoppages occur. Process: diagnosis, re-sharpening if needed, appropriate coating. Article topic: "PVD/CVD coating: when it is truly worth it for your cutting tools."

### August — Summer closure

Ask client for exact closure dates. Message should thank clients and recommend anticipating drop-offs/requests before closure.

### October — Collection and delivery

Position the service as a way to reduce hidden logistics cost, travel, interruptions, and deadline uncertainty. Process: initial contact, tool collection, workshop intervention, planned return. Link: https://www.desclouxsa.ch/livraison/

### December — Winter greetings and closure

Ask client for exact closure and reopening dates. Thank clients, partners, and suppliers for trust. Encourage anticipation of drop-offs/requests before closure.
