# Albert Descloux SA — personas

## Wood workshop manager

Works with circular saw blades, panels, or solid wood. Needs cleaner cuts, less chipping, and reliable sharpening.

## Machining / mechanical workshop operator

Notices overheating, vibration, noise, burrs, or surface quality issues. Needs a practical diagnosis and tool maintenance guidance.

## Production manager

Wants to reduce downtime, rejects, logistics burden, and tool replacement frequency.

## Client needing logistics support

Values collection and delivery because tool drop-offs and pickups interrupt workshop productivity.
