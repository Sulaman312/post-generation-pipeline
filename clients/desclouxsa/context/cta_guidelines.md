# Albert Descloux SA — CTA guidelines

## CTA approach

CTAs should invite a diagnosis, quote, or practical contact. Keep them technical, useful, and direct.

## Recommended CTAs

- Need a diagnosis? Send us a photo of your tool and the symptom.
- Contact us for advice.
- Ask for the collection and delivery procedure.
- Request an opinion on your tool.
- Send the material, tool type, and symptom.
