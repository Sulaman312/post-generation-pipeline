# Schneiter et Fils — writing guidelines

## Style rules

- Write soberly and clearly.
- Prefer simple sentences and practical guidance.
- Never dramatize grief.
- Avoid promotional language.
- Use neutral explanations for burial, cremation, formalities, and remembrance.
- Keep emojis minimal and appropriate when they appear in social content.

## Social content

- Facebook / Instagram: humane, simple, supportive, suitable for families.
- LinkedIn: professional, structured, clear, and respectful.
- Google Business: concise, useful, and reassuring.

## Sensitive-topic rules

- Do not imply there is one right way to grieve.
- Do not pressure people to act before they are ready, except for practical urgent contact information.
- Avoid fear-based framing around death, administrative burden, or family decisions.
