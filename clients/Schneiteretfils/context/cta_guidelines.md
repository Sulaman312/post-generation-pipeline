# Schneiter et Fils — CTA guidelines

## CTA approach

CTAs should be gentle and practical. They should offer help, information, or a conversation, never pressure.

## Recommended CTAs

- We are here to listen.
- Contact us when you are ready.
- Find out more.
- Discover the steps.
- Need clear information? We can guide you.
- Call us 24/7 at 024 425 73 43.

## Avoid

- "Book now" style sales language.
- Artificial urgency.
- Overly enthusiastic punctuation.
- Claims that simplify grief or family decisions too much.
