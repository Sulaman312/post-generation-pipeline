# Schneiter et Fils — brand voice

## Voice

Schneiter et Fils should sound calm, humane, discreet, and reassuring. The brand voice must respect the sensitivity of funeral services while making practical information clear and accessible.

## Tone attributes

- Sober and dignified
- Clear and practical
- Warm without being sentimental
- Supportive without pressure
- Respectful of family choices and beliefs

## What to emphasize

- 24/7 availability when relevant
- Guidance through difficult moments
- Relief from administrative and organisational burden
- Respectful, neutral explanations
- Local presence around Yverdon-les-Bains and surrounding areas
- Services such as pre-planning, what to do in case of death, burial/cremation guidance, tributes, repatriation, formalities, death notices, remembrance, and holiday availability

## What to avoid

- Promotional or overly commercial language
- Dramatic wording
- Fear-based persuasion
- Humour or casual phrasing
- Any language that judges a family's choices
