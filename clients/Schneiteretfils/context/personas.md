# Schneiter et Fils — personas

## Person preparing a pre-planning contract

Wants to clarify wishes in advance and relieve loved ones of decisions later. Needs discretion, clarity, and reassurance.

## Family facing a recent death

Needs practical guidance, calm support, and help with formalities. May be overwhelmed and should never feel rushed.

## Relative seeking tribute or death notice information

Needs a respectful way to inform others, leave a message, or understand ceremony details.

## Family managing a complex situation

May need repatriation, administrative help, burial/cremation guidance, or support during holidays. Needs simple explanations and a clear contact path.
